### **2. Showcase Grids**
To create a grid of items inside a page, just use a standard list with your data separated by pipes (`|`).
```text
- Robot Arm | assets/arm.glb | 3D Simulation | assets/thumb.jpg
- Web App   | https://site.com | React Portfolio | assets/web.jpg
```
*The engine sees the pipes inside a list and automatically builds a premium Bento grid.*

### **3. Galleries (Albums)**
To show a set of photos or models:
`Album: img1.jpg, img2.jpg, img3.jpg`
*Or just separate them with commas. The system handles the rest.*
