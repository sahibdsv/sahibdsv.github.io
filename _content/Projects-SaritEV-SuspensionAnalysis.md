## Trailer Suspension

Independent suspension system for urban terrain. MacPherson strut with adaptive damping.

## Design Parameters

Travel: 80mm, spring rate: 2.8 N/mm. [https://placehold.co/400/dc2626/FFF?text=Strut+Assembly, https://placehold.co/400/ef4444/FFF?text=Kinematic+Model] Optimized for 25kg payload at 30km/h over 50mm obstacles.

## Testing Results

Accelerometer data from 200km test routes. Peak vertical acceleration: 0.8g. [https://placehold.co/350/f87171/FFF?text=Acceleration+Plot, https://placehold.co/350/fca5a5/FFF?text=Frequency+Response, https://placehold.co/350/fecaca/FFF?text=Durability+Test] Validated ISO 2631-1 comfort standards.
