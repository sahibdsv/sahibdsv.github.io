Custom 1U payload compartment with electromagnetic shielding and active thermal control.

## Shielding

Aluminum honeycomb panels with 40dB attenuation at 2.4GHz.

## Mounting System

Iso-damper mounts (6 DOF, 15Hz natural frequency). Reduced payload vibration by 68% during launch simulation.
