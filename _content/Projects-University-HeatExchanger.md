## Shell & Tube Design

Thermal design project for MECH 3201. Counter-flow heat exchanger with 85% effectiveness.

## Thermal Calculations

NTU-effectiveness method. Surface area: 12.5 m². [https://placehold.co/400/ef4444/FFF?text=Temperature+Profile, https://placehold.co/400/f87171/FFF?text=LMTD+Diagram] Heat duty: 45 kW with 15°C approach.

## Mechanical Design

316 stainless steel tubes (19mm OD, 1.2mm wall). [https://placehold.co/350/fca5a5/FFF?text=Tube+Bundle, https://placehold.co/350/fecaca/FFF?text=Baffle+Layout, https://placehold.co/350/fee2e2/FFF?text=Shell+Assembly] Pressure drop: 18 kPa (shell), 12 kPa (tube).
