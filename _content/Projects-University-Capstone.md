## Autonomous Delivery Robot

Final year capstone project. ROS-based navigation with object detection and lift mechanism.

## System Integration

Differential drive platform with LIDAR SLAM. [https://placehold.co/450/22c55e/FFF?text=Robot+CAD, https://placehold.co/450/4ade80/FFF?text=Electronics+Bay] Payload capacity: 5kg, range: 2km per charge.

## Achievements

Demonstrated autonomous delivery across York campus. [https://placehold.co/300/86efac/FFF?text=Path+Recording, https://placehold.co/300/bbf7d0/FFF?text=Detection+Demo, https://placehold.co/300/dcfce7/FFF?text=Team+Photo] Received Best Design Award from department.
