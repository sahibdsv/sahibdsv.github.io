## Power Distribution

EPS design with triple-junction solar cells and Li-ion battery management.

## Solar Array

29.5% efficient GaAs cells. Peak generation: 18.5W (EOL). [https://placehold.co/450/065f46/FFF?text=Cell+Layout, https://placehold.co/450/047857/FFF?text=I-V+Curve] 4-panel deployable configuration.

## Battery System

18650 cells (8S2P, 29.6V nominal, 6800mAh). BMS with cell balancing and over-temperature protection. [https://placehold.co/300/0891b2/FFF?text=BMS+Board, https://placehold.co/300/06b6d4/FFF?text=Battery+Pack, https://placehold.co/300/22d3ee/FFF?text=Thermal+Model] 450 orbital cycles validated.
