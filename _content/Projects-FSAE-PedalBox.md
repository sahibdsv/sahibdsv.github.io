## Pedal Assembly

Ergonomic pedal design with adjustable pedal ratio and integrated load cells.

## Mechanical Design

6061-T6 aluminum construction. Pedal ratio: 4.5:1 with three adjustment positions. [https://placehold.co/400/c2410c/FFF?text=Pedal+Assembly, https://placehold.co/400/ea580c/FFF?text=Mounting+Bracket] TIG welded joints with 1.5 safety factor.

## Load Sensing

250kg load cells at master cylinder input. [https://placehold.co/350/fb923c/FFF?text=Sensor+Installation, https://placehold.co/350/fdba74/FFF?text=Calibration+Data, https://placehold.co/350/fed7aa/FFF?text=CAN+Output] Data logged at 100Hz for driver feedback analysis.
