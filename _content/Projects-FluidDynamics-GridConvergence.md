## Mesh Independence Study

Richardson extrapolation and GCI analysis for CFD solution verification.

## Methodology

Three mesh levels: 500K, 2M, 8M cells. [https://placehold.co/400/0891b2/FFF?text=Coarse+Mesh, https://placehold.co/400/06b6d4/FFF?text=Medium+Mesh] Refinement ratio: 1.5 in all directions.

## Convergence Results

GCI for exit Mach: 1.2%. Asymptotic convergence confirmed. [https://placehold.co/350/22d3ee/FFF?text=Convergence+Plot, https://placehold.co/350/67e8f9/FFF?text=Error+Distribution] Adopted 2M mesh for production runs.
