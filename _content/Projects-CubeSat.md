Structural and component design for a research satellite prototype. Validated for launch loads and orbital thermal conditions.

## Structure & Analysis

Designed the primary structure using <i>SolidWorks</i>. Conducted structural analysis in <i>Ansys</i> to ensure payload integrity under launch vibration and acceleration loads. Contributed to tooling for <u>active space debris removal</u>.

## Thermal Validation

Simulated LEO thermal cycling (-150°C to +120°C) to verify component survivability. Executed FEA validation across 50+ load cases.
