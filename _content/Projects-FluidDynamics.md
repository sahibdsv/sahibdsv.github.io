title: Supersonic Nozzle

CFD study of shock structures and boundary layer separation in a convergent-divergent nozzle using ANSYS Fluent.

## Mesh & Model

Structured hex mesh (2M cells, Y+ < 1). SST turbulence model. 
<div class="inline-gallery"><img src="https://placehold.co/500/312e81/FFF?text=Mesh+Detail" class="inline-img zoomable" loading="lazy" alt="Gallery Image"><img src="https://placehold.co/500/3730a3/FFF?text=Boundary+Layer" class="inline-img zoomable" loading="lazy" alt="Gallery Image"></div>
 Convergence at 850 iterations.

## Results

Captured normal shock at throat and oblique shocks in diverging section. Exit Mach: 2.4. Pressure recovery: 87%.

## Validation

<5% error against wind tunnel data for pressure distribution.
